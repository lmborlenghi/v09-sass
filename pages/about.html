About me

I am a Photo Retoucher based in Argentina. I started with photography over 16 years ago, and fell in love with Photo Retouching immediately after; so I put the camera down and I´ve been doing it ever since. I love creating sceneries and composites, tweaking, adjusting, grading, painting, exploring, learning new techniques… and boosting the image to another level! Nothing is as satisfying as looking at a before & after. I also have published work on the book “200 Best Digital Artists Worldwide 19/20” by Luerzer´s Archive :)

I work at Media.Monks on simultaneous worldwide projects for brands such as HP, BMW, Coca-Cola, Oreo, Ritz, Toblerone, among other Mondelez brands.

And also for Boogieman Media, where I create imagery for Netflix movies and series: key visuals, billboards, story arts, horizontal and vertical displays arts… I have worked on over 100 projects on the platform.

...I always had curiosity on coding so on 2023, I decided not to postpone it anymore and started studying to become a Front End Developer.
